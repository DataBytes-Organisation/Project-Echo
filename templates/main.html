Animal Image and Audio Tagger

Welcome to our Animal Identification tool! Upload a WAV audio file to identify animal sounds or an image in JPEG/PNG format to recognize animals visually. Our advanced algorithms work separately to provide accurate predictions for each type of input. The InceptionV3 model trained in the imagenet dataset is used for image identification . A combination of CNN and LSTM is used for audion identification.Discover the power of AI in recognizing the animal world through sound and sight.

The system is able to identify the animals like: Dog, Rooster, Pig, Cow, Frog, Cat, Hen, Insects, Sheep, Crow from their sounds. The InceptionV3 model has the ability to identify many varieties of the animals also. Different dataset where used to train the models like E50 for the audio datset and Imagenet for the image identification model.
